About SUUCL ISoc

SUUCL Islamic Society is an affiliate society at Students Union University College London.

Copyright © SUUCL Islamic Society 2017

Alternative Freshers Fortnight

The ISoc provides an alternative freshers schedule for Muslim students on campus, so that they can enjoy a halal experience. Check in on our Facebook page early September to see plans unfold.

Socials

Fortnightly socials are a great end to the week, in the company of your friends with free food provided!

Careers

Frequent careeers talks and workshops are delivered, often by alumni, to give our members the best chance at securing a job upon graduating

Development

There is no time like the present when it comes to developing oneself spiritually, and the Islamic Society does its best to facilitate your growth. With a combination of regular classes topped up with flagship events hosted by world reknown scholars, there is a great chance to further yourself.

Charity Week

Charity Week is the flagship event of the ISoc year. Last year we raised over £100,000 for Islamic Relief and we hope to beat that total this coming year!

Get involved!

The society is run fully by volunteers, and it can always do with more! Get involved whether it be shopping for Happy Hour or bucketing for Charity Week!
